The joint probabilities for each pair are given by the following table.
\begin{center}
\begin{tabular}{|c|c|c|c|}

\hline $Y=0$ & 0.1  & 0.4 & 0.1 \\ 
\hline  $Y=1$ & 0.1 & 0.1 & 0.2 \\ 
\hline 
\end{tabular} 
\end{center}
\begin{itemize}

\end{itemize}
